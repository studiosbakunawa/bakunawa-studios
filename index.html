The Abyss

Ancient legends tell of the Bakunawa, the great sea serpent who devoured the seven celestial moons. But what mortals perceived as an end was, in fact, a metamorphosis. The light was not extinguished; it was taken into the abyss to be reborn.

We are the inheritors of this cycle. At Bakunawa Studios, we delve into the creative darkness to find the brightest ideas, taking familiar concepts like tower defense and transforming them into something unexpected. From version 0.1's blue void to our current beautiful chaos, we work with ancient patience and modern precision, forging sparks of inspiration into complete, living universes.

But here's what makes our journey special: we're building this with you. Your feedback shapes our worlds, your suggestions breathe life into our creatures, and your creativity helps us name our creations. Come join us in the abyss, together, we'll create something extraordinary.
